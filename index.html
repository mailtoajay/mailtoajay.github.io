Muditha (A Non Profit Organization)

Mudita according to teachings of Buddha, means pure joy especially sympathetic joy we experience by delighting in other people's well-being without any self-interest. Simply put, it is a state of mind in which we are happy when others are happy. The initiative “ Mudita “ by a Trust in India has been working in India for over 8 years, focusing on education with more emphasis on education of girl child. Every girl who is educated, healthy, and empowered becomes a catalyst for change for her family, her community and her nation. Education of any child and girls in particular will be more effective in changing the world for better.

Mudita helps schools to improve their infrastructure and resources to offer quality education to children from poor and vulnerable sections of the society. The contribution comes in various forms depending on the needs of children studying in schools with limited resources. This includes providing smart class rooms, benches, uniforms, books and cycles, renovation of buildings, construction of toilets, provision of drinking water, dining facility, science labs and sports facilities, payment of school fees and scholarships for higher education. We help girl children from poor and marginalised communities with limited means get access to quality education.
